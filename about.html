Our Story

A TRUSTED NAME IN LUPERÓN

Tropical Marina has served the cruising community for decades, providing a safe haven for sailors navigating the Caribbean. Located deep inside Luperón Bay—one of the most protected natural harbors in the region—our marina has become synonymous with quality service, skilled workmanship, and genuine hospitality.

What began as a small operation has grown into a full-service marina and boatyard, yet we've never lost sight of what matters most: treating every vessel with care and every crew like family. Our roots in this community run deep, and that connection shapes everything we do.

Today, Tropical Marina continues to evolve, with plans underway for a 60-ton hydraulic boat lift to complement our proven tractor-and-trailer haul-out system. Through every upgrade, our commitment remains the same: providing cruisers with the safe, reliable, and welcoming service they deserve.

Strategic Location

WHY LUPERÓN BAY

Luperón Bay sits on the northern coast of the Dominican Republic, in the Puerto Plata province. Its unique geography—a deep, enclosed natural harbor wrapped by hills and dense mangrove forests—creates one of the Caribbean's most sheltered anchorages.

For cruising sailors, this means calm waters, minimal surge, and exceptional protection during hurricane season. The bay's reputation has made it a trusted stop for international cruisers for over 30 years.

  • One of the Caribbean's safest natural hurricane holes
  • Protected on all sides by hills and mangroves
  • Calm, minimal-surge anchorage year-round
  • Walking distance to town amenities and services
  • Established cruising community and support network
