Welcome to Tropical Marina

SAFE HARBOR & EXPERT CARE

Tropical Marina is located deep inside Luperón Bay, one of the safest hurricane anchorages in the Caribbean. Our marina sits in a protected basin wrapped by mangroves and hills, providing exceptional natural shelter that cruisers have trusted for decades.

Just a short walk from the town center of Luperón, our guests enjoy easy access to restaurants, hardware stores, grocery shops, and local services—making Tropical Marina the ideal base for both extended stays and essential boat work.

Strategic Location

WHY LUPERÓN BAY

Luperón Bay has earned its reputation as one of the Caribbean's premier hurricane holes. The deep, enclosed natural harbor is protected on all sides by hills and dense mangrove forests, creating calm waters with minimal surge even during severe weather.

For decades, international cruisers have trusted Luperón as their go-to destination for hurricane season storage and essential boat maintenance.

  • Deep, enclosed natural harbor
  • Protected by surrounding hills and mangroves
  • Minimal surge and wave exposure
  • Trusted by cruisers for 30+ years
  • Walking distance to town amenities

World-Class Fishing

FISH THE NORTH COAST

Luperón sits at the gateway to some of the Caribbean's richest fishing grounds. The waters off the Dominican Republic's north coast are home to blue marlin, mahi-mahi, wahoo, and yellowfin tuna—making this an angler's paradise.

Whether you're heading offshore for big game or fishing the bay for snapper and grouper, Tropical Marina is your ideal home base with easy access to open water.
