Find Us

DIRECTIONS

Tropical Marina is located inside Luperon Bay on the northern coast of the Dominican Republic, in the Puerto Plata province. The bay has a well-marked entrance and is known as one of the Caribbean's safest natural harbors.

GPS Coordinates

19°53.68' N, 70°57.23' W
  • Navigate to the Luperon Bay entrance waypoint
  • Follow the marked channel into the inner harbor
  • Contact us on VHF 68 as you approach
  • We'll guide you to your assigned berth or mooring
